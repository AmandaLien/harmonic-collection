Boba of the Week

Sept. 19th, 2023


Teazzi's deep roast oolong tea latte + boba


  • tea: 炭培鐵觀音 deep roast oolong tea
  • style: 鮮奶茶 tea latte
  • toppings: 珍珠 tapioca pearls
  • sugar level: 少糖 30%
  • ice level: 少冰 less ice

  • size: large
  • price: $6.5 + NY tax
  • location: Union Square - 47 West 14th St #3, NY 10017

  • other qualities: paper cup, plastic sealing and straw

  • rating: ◉ ◉ ◉ ◉ ◐

瓷禧 Teazzi never disappoints with their strong and rare tea scents. The taste of their boba pearls is always kept at a high level. However, since I always take a much longer time to finish the drink, I would end up tasting hardening pearls. I just wish the serving time was longer than it’s supposed to be. (not exactly stated, but can be assumed to be 1 hour) At this Union Square location, the service provided by their boba baristas is also very friendly. At the end, I would rate this 4.5 points just because I'd personally like the tea to be even stronger and maybe give them some space for improvement.
