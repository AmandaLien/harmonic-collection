Bobarista Insiders

Nov. 14th, 2023









Special interview

特別訪談

Today, I invited a special guest to join us! I've prepared a series of questions about boba and their job as a boba barista.

(this interview was conducted digitally through texts.)


Are you a fan of bubble tea?

当然是！
Of course!

How often do you drink bubble tea?

4 to 5 times a week!

Did your answer to the questions above affect your decision to become a boba barista?

这倒不是
hmm not really

What are some things you do as a boba barista?

cashier/ adding toppings/摇茶/ 出杯
I can be the cashier, or the barista(add toppings and shake/mix tea), or the one to hand the customers their drinks.

What’s the hardest part of your job?

cashier/出杯 因为要面对客人 有时候有奇怪到对我们很mean的客人
It’s when I’m working as a cashier or handing out the drinks because I have to face the customers. Sometimes there are weird customers that are mean to us.

What are the things you like about being a boba barista?

摇茶的过程很解压哈哈哈哈
The process of shaking and mixing the tea is very stress relieving hahahahaa

Are the any insider or fun facts that you would want to reveal to the other bubble tea lovers reading this?

哈哈哈哈果茶比奶茶糖份多！！！会多加果酱！！不要在人多的时候去点！我们会忙不过来摇的很敷衍
loll the sugar portion is greater in fruit teas when compared to milk teas!!! we just add more fruit jams!! so don’t order them when the stores are busy! we would half ass the shaking when we’re too busy

In your opinion, what elements do you believe are essential in creating a good bubble tea experience?

sweetness！太甜会ruin整杯奶茶
the sweetness! too much sweetness would ruin the whole bubble tea.

What’s your go-to order?

depends 不同时候有不同喜欢的 不过现在更喜欢荔枝红lychee black tea和百香柳橙passionfruit orange！
it depends, my likings change from time to time but my current favorites are lychee black tea and passion fruit orange.

What do you think about the average price of bubble tea being around $7 dollars (large-size) nowadays? Do you think it’s worth it?

看情况！有些饮品不值得 比如蜂蜜柠檬 其实可以自己在家里做！如果是很少见的茶 比如铁观音奶茶/puer的话还是值得的！
it varies! some drinks are not worth the price, like honey lemon because you can actually make it at home your self! However if it’s rarer kind of tea such as tieguanyin/ deep roast oolong tea or puer tea, I think it’s still worth it!

I see! I hope the readers enjoyed these intresting insiders from you!
thank you for joining the entry for this week Xxxxx!

everyday is a boba day~
